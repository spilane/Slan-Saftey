Welcome to site
This app manages your daily WHS compliance — sign in, toolbox talks, pre-starts, hazard reporting and more. Setup takes about 2 minutes.
Daily sign in & out

Safety Stars
Earn points for compliance actions and standout safety behaviour. Monthly leaderboard and prize draws.
Your data — This app collects your name, photo, GPS location at sign in/out, and form submissions for site WHS compliance purposes. Your data is stored securely in Australia, managed by Slán Safety on behalf of your employer, and never sold or shared with third parties. Location is recorded only at the moment you sign in or out.
