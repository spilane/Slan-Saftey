Site Rules & WHS Acknowledgements
Read each group carefully and tick to confirm — Section 10 unlocks on completion
Read each group of obligations carefully. Tick the confirmation box at the bottom of each group before moving to the next. All groups must be confirmed before you can proceed.
WHS Obligations
I understand that I have a duty under the applicable work health and safety legislation to take reasonable care for my own health and safety and the health and safety of others who may be affected by my acts or omissions at work.
I understand that I have the right to cease or refuse unsafe work where I have a reasonable concern that to carry out the work would expose me to a serious risk to my health or safety. This right is protected by law.
I understand that I must comply with any reasonable instruction given by [Company Name], the principal contractor, site management, or their authorised representatives in relation to work health and safety.
I understand that I must not attend site under the influence of alcohol or drugs, including prescription medication that may impair my ability to work safely. I consent to drug and alcohol testing if required.
I understand that I must report all incidents, near misses, hazards, and unsafe conditions immediately using the site reporting system or by notifying my supervisor.
I understand that I must not commence any high risk work without a current, signed and approved Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS) for that activity.
I understand that I must wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) at all times on site as required by [Company Name] site rules and as specified in the relevant SWMS.
I confirm that I have been informed of the minimum PPE requirements for this site and I accept responsibility for presenting to work with the correct PPE.
I understand that I must not operate any plant, equipment, or machinery for which I do not hold the required licence, ticket, or competency — including a Verification of Competency (VOC) where required.
I declare that I am fit for work today — I am not under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or fatigue and I am physically and mentally fit to perform my duties safely.
Site Rules
I understand that mobile phones are to be used in designated areas only and must not be used while operating plant or performing high risk work.
I understand that I must sign in and out of site every day using the site sign-in system and that my attendance is recorded.
I understand that any damage to site, equipment, or property must be reported to my supervisor immediately.
I understand that bullying, harassment, discrimination, or threatening behaviour toward any person on site will result in immediate removal from site.
I understand that the principal contractor's site rules take precedence on this site and that I am required to comply with all site-specific requirements communicated to me.
Psychosocial Safety
I am aware that [Company Name] has a Psychosocial Risk Management system in place and that I can report concerns about bullying, harassment, workload, fatigue, or any other psychosocial hazard confidentially using the site Wellbeing & Concerns portal.
I understand that all psychosocial reports are treated confidentially and that I may report anonymously if I choose.
Environmental Obligations
I understand that I must not dispose of any waste, chemicals, or materials in a manner that may cause harm to the environment, including discharge to stormwater drains or waterways.
I am aware that any environmental incidents — including spills, unexpected contamination, or damage to the environment — must be reported immediately using the site reporting system.
Drug & Alcohol Policy
I acknowledge that I have read and understood the company's Drug & Alcohol Policy. I understand that random testing may occur and that breach of the policy may result in disciplinary action including termination.

Privacy Collection Notice

The personal and health information collected in this form is collected by [Company Name] as the responsible employer and data controller. [Company Name] has appointed Slán Safety Pty Ltd as its authorised WHS compliance administrator to collect and manage this information on its behalf. Slán Safety acts solely as a data processor under instruction from [Company Name] and does not use your information for any independent purpose.

Your information will not be disclosed to any third party — meaning any party outside of [Company Name] and its appointed administrators — without your consent, unless required by law. This includes disclosure to the relevant WHS/OHS regulatory authority in the event of a notifiable incident.

You have the right to access and correct your personal information at any time by contacting your site supervisor or the safety administrator. This form is administered under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).
